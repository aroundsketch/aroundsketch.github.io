Welcome

With private search, Brave will use DuckDuckGo for searches in Private Windows. DuckDuckGo doesn’t track your search history, so you can search privately.

Private Window

Brave doesn’t remember what you do in a Private Window. Sites you visit won't show up in your history, and cookies vanish when you’re done. Private Windows don’t make you completely anonymous online, though.
